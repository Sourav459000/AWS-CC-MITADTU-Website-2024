About Us

The AWS Cloud Club - MIT ADTU is a dynamic and student-driven community dedicated to exploring the boundless possibilities of cloud technology through the lens of Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Our Vision

  • Cultivate a dynamic, student-led community passionate about AWS.
  • Provide expert-guided, in-depth education on Amazon Web Services.
  • Offer practical, hands-on experiences aligning with market trends.

Our Mission

  • Educate students diverse AWS applications: security, AI, etc.
  • Host events and workshops focused on AWS cloud computing.
  • Foster industry connections, providing networking and insights.
  • Enrich understanding through guest speakers' invaluable insights.
